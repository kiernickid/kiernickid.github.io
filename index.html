Stocks and The Presidents

With the 2020 election coming this Fall, two questions you might be asking yourself are "Will one of the candidates cause the stock market to crash? Should I pull out all of my money just in case it crashes?"


The answer to those questions depends on the stocks you're interested in and the industry. I gathered the historical stock prices for the 2019 Fortune 10 companies under the last 4 US Presidents. Some stocks increase rapidly and are volatile. Some give moderate returns with little variability. Click through the slides and configure the graphs to your interest!
